Common questions

Everything you want to know before you call.

Does it really handle emergency plumbing calls? +

Yes. The AI is trained on plumbing-specific terminology (slab leaks, sump pump failures, burst supply lines, pressure-reducing valves, backflow preventers, T&P relief valves). It asks diagnostic questions that sound like a real plumbing receptionist would ask. Try calling the demo and saying "my basement is flooding." You'll see.

Why is it $79/month when Ruby charges $235+? +

Ruby uses human operators. Humans cost money per minute. VoiceClaw uses AI, which doesn't. You get 24/7 coverage at a flat rate instead of variable per-call billing. If we're subsidized through a supply-house partnership you already use (Ferguson Pro, etc.), it can be even less.

Does it lock me to one supply house or fixture brand? +

No. VoiceClaw is supplier-neutral. You buy from Ferguson, Winsupply, Hajoca, Home Depot Pro, or whichever counter you prefer. We don't change what you install or specify. If you later use a Ferguson subsidy benefit, great. But it's not a requirement.

What happens when someone calls my old number? +

You can either keep your existing number and forward overflow/after-hours calls to VoiceClaw, or port your number fully. Most plumbers start with forwarding to test, then port once they trust it. We handle the telecom piece; you don't touch a carrier.

Can it actually book into my existing schedule? +

Yes. VoiceClaw syncs with Google Calendar, Jobber, Housecall Pro, and most field-service tools. If you run on paper or SMS, it can text you the booking details and confirm with the customer directly until you catch up.

How is the 2-minute setup real? Other tools take weeks. +

Other tools make you configure a dashboard. VoiceClaw asks you about your business in a phone conversation and configures itself from what you say. No forms, no logins, no dashboard to learn. If you can describe your plumbing business to a new customer, you can set VoiceClaw up.

Can I cancel? +

Yes. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Your phone number ports back out. We keep your logs and transcripts accessible for 90 days after cancellation.
